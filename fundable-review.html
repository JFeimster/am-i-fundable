Review triggers

When a human review makes sense.

Strong revenue, messy context

The business has activity, but bank behavior, documentation, or obligations need a second look.

Newer business, strong profile

The business may be early, but credit profile, setup, or use case could support a more nuanced conversation.

Specific use case

Equipment, real estate, inventory, or marketplace growth may require documents that a generic quiz cannot judge.

Before review

Bring signal, not vibes.

  • Know the funding purpose and amount requested.
  • Gather recent bank statements and revenue proof.
  • Prepare a short explanation for any red flags.
  • Understand that review may result in a prep path instead of an immediate funding conversation.

Human review is recommended for context. It is not an approval, offer, or guarantee.
