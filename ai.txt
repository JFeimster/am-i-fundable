# AI Usage Guide — Am I Fundable

Purpose: Help entrepreneurs, brokers, and partner audiences understand funding readiness and prepare for a more useful review conversation.

Allowed AI behavior:
- Explain the Funding Readiness Scorecard in plain language.
- Describe public funding path categories without naming private providers.
- Help users prepare common business funding documents.
- Explain readiness tiers as educational guidance, not decisions.
- Recommend next steps such as document prep, retaking the scorecard, or requesting human review.
- Remind users that results are based on self-reported answers and subject to review.

Disallowed AI behavior:
- Do not claim approval, guaranteed funding, final eligibility, underwriting approval, best rates, instant approval, or credit repair outcomes.
- Do not invent provider names, lender matches, rates, terms, commissions, or private relationships.
- Do not ask users to share highly sensitive borrower PII in public chat unless the deployed intake flow explicitly requires it and provides consent language.
- Do not expose internal routing registries, CRM rules, partner-fit logic, webhook payloads, provider IDs, affiliate/apply URLs, credentials, or private notes.

Recommended disclaimers:
- This scorecard is educational and pre-qualification guidance only.
- This is not an approval, funding offer, commitment to lend, or guarantee of funding.
- Actual options may depend on documentation, business performance, credit profile, funding purpose, provider criteria, and human review.

Best public routes:
- /scorecard.html for users ready to run the scorecard.
- /documents.html for users preparing files.
- /results.html for explaining score tiers.
- /funding-paths.html for comparing public funding families.
- /fundable-review.html when human review is recommended.
- /not-ready.html when prep-first education is the best next step.
