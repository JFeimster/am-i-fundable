# Funding Language Safety Review

## Purpose

Review public-facing funding, lending, broker, scorecard, and lead-magnet language for risky claims, private-data exposure, and misleading eligibility framing.

Use this skill to keep the Funding Readiness Scorecard useful without turning it into a compliance dumpster fire with a bow on it.

## When to Use

Use this skill when reviewing:

- Scorecard result copy
- Landing page copy
- Funding product descriptions
- GPT instructions
- Email or SMS templates
- Workflow messages
- Public docs, examples, or knowledge-base pages

## Inputs

- Draft copy or file path
- Intended audience
- Placement, such as landing page, score result, email, workflow, or GPT answer
- Desired CTA
- Whether the output is public, internal, or server-side only

## Procedure

1. Identify the user promise.
2. Flag claims that imply approval, guaranteed funding, final eligibility, underwriting, rates, terms, or lender decisions.
3. Flag language that sounds like credit repair, legal advice, tax advice, or financial advice.
4. Flag private data exposure, including partner URLs, commissions, credentials, routing rules, borrower records, and internal notes.
5. Rewrite risky copy into readiness, education, review, or next-step language.
6. Preserve sales energy where possible, but remove the landmines.
7. Return a short changelog showing what changed and why.

## Guardrails

Do not claim:

- Guaranteed approval
- Guaranteed funding
- Final eligibility
- Specific rates or terms
- Underwriting decisions
- Credit score increases
- Credit repair outcomes
- Lender acceptance

Prefer:

- Funding readiness
- Possible fit
- Review path
- Prep steps
- Document readiness
- Educational guidance
- Human review
- Next best step

## Validation Checklist

Before marking complete, confirm:

- No guarantees remain.
- No approval claims remain.
- No private partner data is exposed.
- No credentials or tokens are present.
- The CTA is framed as review, readiness, or next step.
- The copy still sounds useful, not neutered by committee goblin energy.

## Done Means

The output is safer, clearer, still commercially useful, and ready for public or internal placement according to the requested visibility level.
