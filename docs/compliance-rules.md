# Funding Readiness Compliance Rules

## Purpose

These rules keep the Funding Readiness Scorecard useful, conversion-focused, and safe.

The scorecard is an educational pre-screening and routing experience. It is not a lender decision engine.

## Required result disclaimer

> This scorecard is for educational and pre-screening guidance only. It is not a lending decision, commitment to lend, or funding offer. Actual options depend on documentation, lender criteria, business performance, credit profile, bank activity, and other review factors.

## Consent copy

> I agree to be contacted about funding options and understand this score is not a lending decision, funding offer, or commitment to lend.

## Preferred language

- Funding readiness
- Potential funding path
- May be a fit for review
- Based on your answers
- Recommended next step
- Funding strategy review
- Document review
- Funding path match
- Manual review recommended
- Business funding options

## Avoid risky language

- Certainty around outcomes
- Lending-decision language
- Universal access claims
- Rate promises
- Timeline promises that sound final
- Credit outcome promises
- Statements that imply underwriting is complete

## Human review triggers

Send a lead to human review if any of the following are present:

- Open bankruptcy signal
- Tax lien signal
- Recent missed payments
- Recent NSFs or overdrafts
- Recently opened business bank account
- Existing daily-payment advance
- Suspended marketplace account
- No current revenue
- Requested amount appears materially higher than revenue support
- Conflicting answers
