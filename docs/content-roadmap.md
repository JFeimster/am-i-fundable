# Content Roadmap

This roadmap turns the Funding Readiness Scorecard into a public education and lead-capture ecosystem.

The goal is not “more pages because the internet was feeling lonely.” The goal is targeted content that moves users from confusion to readiness to review.

## Content pillars

1. Funding readiness education.
2. Document prep.
3. Funding paths by use case.
4. Persona-specific readiness.
5. Broker/partner education.
6. Low-readiness nurture.
7. Scorecard and embed usage.

## Internal linking plan

Every education article should link to:

- `/scorecard.html`
- `/documents.html`
- `/funding-paths.html`
- `/faq.html`

Persona pages should link to relevant funding-family pages and document checklists.

Low-readiness pages should link to prep content, not aggressive apply CTAs.

## Safe SEO language

Use:

```txt
funding readiness
business funding preparation
funding document checklist
potential funding paths
review recommended
business financing education
```

Avoid:

```txt
guaranteed approval
instant approval
bad credit approval guaranteed
best business loan rates
no-doc guaranteed funding
credit repair guaranteed
```

## Content QA

Before publishing:

- [ ] Does the content route users to a safe next step?
- [ ] Does it avoid approval or guarantee claims?
- [ ] Does it avoid fake lender specificity?
- [ ] Does it avoid provider names?
- [ ] Does it include a scorecard CTA?
- [ ] Does it link to documents or funding-path education?
- [ ] Does it match the result-language guide?

## Maintenance cadence

Monthly:

- Review FAQ performance.
- Add questions from support/review calls.
- Update resource hub.
- Check internal links.
- Confirm claims remain safe.
- Confirm no provider details leaked into public content.

Quarterly:

- Revisit persona priorities.
- Update lead magnets.
- Audit pages for outdated CTAs.
- Refresh docs based on actual workflow changes.
