# Funding Routing Examples

These examples illustrate how specific answers in the scorecard map to potential recommended paths.

## Example 1: Established Revenue

**Inputs:**
- Time in Business: 2+ Years
- Monthly Revenue: $50,000+
- Credit: Strong

**Potential Match Logic:**
- This profile demonstrates strong fundamentals.
- **Recommended Path:** May be a fit for a comprehensive document review and traditional funding path match.

## Example 2: Pre-Revenue Startup

**Inputs:**
- Time in Business: < 6 Months
- Monthly Revenue: $0
- Credit: Variable

**Potential Match Logic:**
- This profile is in the early stages and lacks the revenue typically required for traditional business funding.
- **Recommended Path:** Focus on business fundamentals, startup resources, or establishing initial revenue streams before seeking growth capital.

## Example 3: Disqualifier Triggered (Manual Review)

**Inputs:**
- Recent Missed Payments: Yes
- Open Bankruptcy: Yes

**Potential Match Logic:**
- Disqualifying signals are present.
- **Recommended Path:** Route to a manual human review to better understand the specific situation. Do not proceed to automated matching.
