# Funding Context

This folder contains knowledge related to the types of funding, readiness signals, and how the scorecard recommends next steps based on applicant input.

## Core Concepts

The scorecard assesses a business's current state based on attributes like:
- Monthly Revenue
- Time in Business
- Credit Profile
- Industry

It uses these signals to recommend a "funding readiness" path, identifying whether the business may be a fit for specific types of review, or if they need to focus on building fundamentals (e.g., establishing revenue).

## Compliance Reminder

- The scorecard provides **educational pre-screening and routing**.
- It is **not a lender decision engine**.
- Use language like "potential funding path", "may be a fit", or "recommended next step".
- **Do not** use terms like "approved", "guaranteed", or "qualified".
