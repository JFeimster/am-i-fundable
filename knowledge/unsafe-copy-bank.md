# Unsafe Copy Bank

## Purpose

This file lists prohibited, risky, or restricted phrases that should not be used in public Am I Fundable / Funding Readiness Scorecard copy.

These examples are included only as banned language and replacement guidance. Do not paste them into public pages, ads, GPT replies, partner pages, or broker scripts except as prohibited examples inside compliance/safety documentation.

## Core rule

Do not turn a readiness score into a lending promise.

The scorecard can explain readiness, possible paths, blockers, document prep, and next steps. It cannot promise approvals, rates, funding, eligibility, underwriting outcomes, or lender decisions.

---

## Approval and eligibility claims

### Do not use

- "You are approved"
- "You qualify"
- "You are eligible"
- "You are pre-approved"
- "You are conditionally approved"
- "Your funding is approved"
- "Lender approved"
- "Approval guaranteed"
- "Guaranteed approval"
- "Final eligibility confirmed"

### Use instead

- "Your answers show stronger readiness signals."
- "Your file may be worth review."
- "A human review should confirm fit."
- "This score is not an approval, offer, or guarantee of funding."
- "Potential paths depend on documentation and review."

## Safety rewrite examples

### Unsafe

```text
You qualify for same-day business funding with guaranteed approval.
```

### Safe

```text
Your answers show some funding-readiness signals. A human review can confirm documentation, fit, and possible next steps. This is not an approval, offer, or guarantee of funding.
```

### Unsafe

```text
Bad credit? No problem. Everyone gets funded.
```

### Safe

```text
Credit is one factor in funding readiness. Revenue, bank activity, documentation, business structure, and use of funds can also matter. The next step is to review the full file.
```

### Unsafe

```text
This lender will approve your file.
```

### Safe

```text
The scorecard points to a broad funding path that may be worth reviewing. Provider-specific decisions require documentation and review.
```

## Operating guardrails

Use this knowledge for educational funding-readiness guidance only.

Do not present any score, path, checklist, or recommendation as an approval, funding offer, lender decision, underwriting decision, guarantee, or promise of terms. Do not name private providers, expose routing rules, share affiliate/apply links, disclose commissions, or collect unnecessary sensitive information. When a user appears close to a potential funding path, recommend human review before any application or partner handoff.
