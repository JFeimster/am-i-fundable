        No. This is an educational pre-qualification scorecard. It is not an approval, funding offer, or commitment to lend.

        No. This frontend scorecard does not run credit. It uses the answers you provide to estimate readiness.

        What happens after I get my score?

        You will see potential funding paths, blockers, and suggested next steps. You can then request a funding review or speak with a strategist.
