# Funding Preparation Checklist

Whether you are just starting out or looking to strengthen your profile, being prepared is key when exploring potential funding paths. This checklist is designed to help you organize your business information.

## 1. Organize Your Financials
- [ ] **Business Bank Statements:** Gather your statements for the last 3 to 6 months. Consistent revenue shown here is often a key factor.
- [ ] **Tax Returns:** Have your most recent business and, when requested, personal tax returns available.
- [ ] **Profit & Loss Statement (P&L):** Prepare a current P&L to show your business's financial health.

## 2. Review Your Credit Profile
- [ ] **Personal Credit Score:** Check your personal credit profile so you know what a provider may review.
- [ ] **Business Credit:** Check your business credit profile and correct any inaccurate information you find through the appropriate reporting channel.

## 3. Business Documentation
- [ ] **Business License/Registration:** Make sure your business is legally registered and in good standing in your state.
- [ ] **Clear Purpose:** Clearly define your funding purpose. Knowing exactly how much you need and what it will be used for can help align you with the right potential options.

## 4. Next Steps for Improvement
If your current readiness score is in a lower tier, consider:
- Building more consistent monthly revenue.
- Reviewing your credit profile for accuracy.
- Organizing documents before starting a full funding conversation.

*Disclaimer: Completing this checklist does not create a funding offer or promise of funding. These are general preparation steps.*
